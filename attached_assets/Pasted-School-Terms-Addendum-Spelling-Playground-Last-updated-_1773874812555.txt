School Terms Addendum

Spelling Playground

Last updated: March 17, 2026

This School Terms Addendum (“Addendum”) supplements and is incorporated into the Terms and Conditions for Spelling Playground (the “Terms”). In the event of a conflict between this Addendum and the Terms, this Addendum will control with respect to School Accounts.

This Addendum applies when the Service is used by a school, school district, or other educational institution (“School”).

1. Role of the Company

Spelling Playground LLC (“Company”) acts as a service provider (or “school official”) to the School.

The Company:

Processes student data solely on behalf of and under the direction of the School

Uses student data only to provide and improve the Service

Does not use student data for advertising or commercial profiling

2. Student Data Ownership and Control

As between the Company and the School:

The School retains ownership and control of all student data

The Company has no ownership rights in student data

The Company accesses and processes student data only as necessary to provide the Service

The School is responsible for:

Determining what student data is submitted to the Service

Managing access to student accounts

Authorizing teachers, staff, and administrators

3. Compliance with Student Privacy Laws

The Company supports the School’s compliance with applicable laws, including:

The Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act

The Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (where applicable)

The Company agrees that:

Student data is used only for educational purposes

Student data is not sold or rented

Student data is not used for targeted advertising

4. Parental Consent

When the Service is used in a school context:

The School may act as the parent’s agent in providing consent for student use of the Service, as permitted under applicable law

The School is responsible for obtaining any required parental notices and consents

The Company will:

Provide information reasonably requested by the School to support parental notice requirements

5. Use of Student Data

The Company uses student data only to:

Provide spelling and educational functionality

Maintain and improve the Service

Ensure security and integrity of the platform

Comply with legal obligations

The Company does not:

Build profiles for non-educational purposes

Use student data for marketing

Share student data except as described in the Terms and Privacy Policy

6. Data Security

The Company implements reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to:

Protect student data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse

Maintain the confidentiality and integrity of student data

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Student data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the Service

The School may request deletion of student data at any time

Upon termination of the School’s use of the Service, the Company will delete student data within a reasonable timeframe, unless retention is required by law

8. Subprocessors

The Company may use trusted third-party service providers (“subprocessors”) to operate the Service (e.g., hosting, payment processing).

The Company ensures that:

Subprocessors are bound by data protection obligations

They access student data only as necessary to provide their services

9. Access and Correction

The School has the right to:

Request access to student data

Request correction or deletion of inaccurate data

Parents may exercise these rights through the School or as described in the Privacy Policy.

10. Content Created by Students

Students and teachers may create content within the Service (such as word lists or images).

Such content may be shared within the Service as part of its educational functionality

The School is responsible for determining appropriate sharing settings

The Company does not use student-created content for advertising or non-educational purposes.

11. Account Management

The School is responsible for:

Managing teacher and student access

Ensuring accounts are used appropriately

Supervising student use of the Service

The Company may suspend accounts if necessary to:

Protect student safety

Prevent misuse

Comply with legal obligations

12. Changes to This Addendum

The Company may update this Addendum from time to time.

If changes are material, the Company will provide reasonable notice to the School.

Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of the updated Addendum.
