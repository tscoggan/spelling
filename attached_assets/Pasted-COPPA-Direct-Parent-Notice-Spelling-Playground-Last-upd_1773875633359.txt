Notice to Parents

Spelling Playground is an educational app designed for children to practice spelling and language skills under the supervision of a parent, legal guardian, or school.

This notice explains how we collect, use, and protect information about children.

1. Information We Collect About Children

We collect only the information necessary to provide the Service.

This may include:

Account Information

Child’s first name or nickname

Username and password

Educational Content

Custom word lists created by parents, teachers, or students

Progress and performance data (e.g., quiz results)

Technical Information

Device type, browser type, and general usage data

IP address (used for security and service functionality)

We do not require children to provide more information than is reasonably necessary to use the Service.

2. How We Use Children’s Information

We use this information only to:

Provide and operate the app

Track learning progress

Improve educational features

Maintain security and prevent misuse

We do not:

Sell children’s personal information

Use children’s data for advertising

Show targeted or behavioral ads to children

3. When Information Is Shared

We do not share children’s personal information except:

With service providers that help us operate the app (e.g., hosting, payments), under strict confidentiality obligations

When required by law or to protect safety

With a school or teacher, if the account is part of a school program

4. Parental Rights

As a parent or legal guardian, you have the right to:

Review your child’s personal information

Request correction of inaccurate information

Request deletion of your child’s information

Refuse further collection or use of your child’s information

You can exercise these rights by contacting us at:
📧 support@spellingplayground.com

5. Parental Consent

We require that a parent or legal guardian create and manage any account used by a child.

By creating an account and allowing your child to use Spelling Playground, you consent to the collection and use of your child’s information as described in this notice and our Privacy Policy.

For school-based use, the school may provide consent on behalf of parents, as permitted by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain children’s information only as long as necessary to:

Provide the Service

Maintain educational records

Comply with legal obligations

You may request deletion of your child’s data at any time.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect children’s information, including:

Secure data transmission (encryption)

Restricted access to personal data

Monitoring for unauthorized access

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this notice or your child’s information, you can contact us:

📧 support@spellingplayground.com
