Yes — they absolutely need to be displayed inside the app, and certain ones must be affirmatively acknowledged during account creation.

For school accounts, this is not optional from a risk perspective.

Below is a clear, implementation-focused breakdown for you and your dev team.

---

# 1️⃣ Which Documents Must Be Actively Accepted?

For School Accounts, you need **clickwrap acceptance** (checkbox + required action) for:

### ✅ School Terms of Service

### ✅ Student Data Privacy Addendum

### ✅ COPPA School Certification Statement

These must be acknowledged during **School Admin account creation**.

You are relying on the school consent exception under the
Children's Online Privacy Protection Act — so the certification step is critical.

---

# 2️⃣ Which Documents Must Be Displayed But Not Necessarily Signed?

These must be accessible but don’t require separate signatures:

* Full Privacy Policy
* Data Deletion Policy
* FERPA compliance section (included within Terms or Privacy Policy)

FERPA relates to the
Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act
but it does not require a separate signature — it requires contractual acknowledgment, which your School Terms already provide.

---

# 3️⃣ Where Should These Appear in the App?

## A. During School Admin Account Creation (Mandatory)

Your school signup flow should include:

### Step 1 — School Information

* School name
* School address
* School domain email required
* Role confirmation

### Step 2 — Legal Acceptance Page (Dedicated Screen)

Display:

* Summary paragraph explaining legal responsibility
* Links to:

  * School Terms (full document)
  * Student Data Privacy Addendum
  * Privacy Policy

Then require:

☐ I have read and agree to the School Terms of Service
☐ I agree to the Student Data Privacy Addendum
☐ I certify that I am authorized to consent on behalf of this School and that required parental consents have been obtained

No pre-checked boxes.
No passive acceptance.
User cannot proceed without checking all required boxes.

This is enforceable clickwrap.

---

## B. Logged Acceptance (Critical)

Upon submission, your system must log:

* User ID
* School ID
* Agreement versions
* Timestamp (UTC)
* IP address
* User agent
* Certification flag
* Hash of agreement text version

These logs should be immutable.

---

## C. Inside the App (Ongoing Access)

After account creation:

You should provide a permanent “Legal” or “Policies” section in:

* Admin dashboard footer
* Settings > Legal
* Public website footer

Schools must be able to:

* Re-access documents
* View current versions
* View effective date

---

# 4️⃣ Do Teachers Need to Accept Anything?

Yes — but lighter weight.

When a teacher account is created:

Require:

☐ I agree to the Teacher Terms
☐ I agree to use Student Data solely for educational purposes

Log it the same way.

Teachers do NOT need to re-certify COPPA authority. That remains with the School.

---

# 5️⃣ Do Students Need to Accept Anything?

For school-managed student accounts:

No.

Students:

* Should not see legal contracts
* Should not agree to terms
* Should not create accounts

The contract is between Company and School.

---

# 6️⃣ Do You Need Digital Signatures?

No, not initially.

Clickwrap is legally enforceable if:

* The terms are clearly displayed or linked
* The user must affirmatively check boxes
* Acceptance is required before proceeding
* Acceptance is logged

You do NOT need:

* DocuSign
* Typed name fields
* PDF signatures
* Identity verification

Those are only needed for district enterprise procurement.

---

# 7️⃣ Should Documents Be Public on Your Website?

Yes.

Even for school-specific terms, best practice is:

* Publicly accessible School Terms URL
* Public Privacy Policy URL
* Public Data Deletion Policy URL

Regulators expect transparency.

---

# 8️⃣ When Must Re-Acceptance Be Required?

You must force re-acceptance if:

* You materially change data use
* You change ownership rights
* You introduce new data collection categories
* You add advertising

For minor wording changes, you may not need re-acceptance — but version tracking must continue.

---

# 9️⃣ Recommended School Account Workflow (Clean & Low-Risk)

1. Admin enters school email
2. Email verification
3. Legal acceptance screen (dedicated page)
4. Certification checkbox required
5. Acceptance logged
6. School account created
7. Teachers invited
8. Students created by teacher only

No bypasses.

---

# 🔐 Implementation Summary for Dev Team

Must Implement:

* Dedicated legal acceptance page in school signup
* Required clickwrap checkboxes
* Versioned agreement storage
* Immutable logging
* Public legal document URLs
* In-app Legal section
* Re-acceptance mechanism

Must NOT Implement:

* Student self-registration
* Student legal acceptance
* Public posting by student accounts
* Ads in school accounts

---

# 🚨 If You Skip Acceptance Logging

If a parent complains and you cannot prove:

* The school certified consent
* The school agreed to terms
* The admin was authorized

You lose the legal shield of the COPPA school exception.

Logging is your protection.
